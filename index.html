Welcome to Bath Safe Routes Map

Whether you're a newcomer to Bath or an expert on Baths streets this map aims to help you get home safely. Perhaps you go out partying? Or maybe you just stay indoors? Either way, the map shows the best way to walk home. It won't show you a path to your house though! The paths are guidelines and with time you'll probably figure out your own path and be familiar with the safe routes. The map will show you key stops along your route. Whether that be a toilet (to avoid the bushes...), a food stop or a cash machine to get some money for a Taxi.

Finally for our inflight annoucement. In the unlikely event of an emergency the locations of River Safety Cabinets and defibrillators are shown when needed. Just click SOS.
